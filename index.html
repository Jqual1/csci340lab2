THE DUALITY OF A KNOT

The knot. A knot is used to make things more secure. A knot can be used to tie a rope to itself or to tie two ropes together. The better the knot the more secure it is. But if the knot is in a hose, then the situation can be dire. Knots in a hose can stop the flow of water. A good knot can keep you attached to a rope, dangling safety over the side of a building. A bad knot will make you come lose and fall. As a firefighter, you must know the following things.

    Firefighters must:

  • make sure there are no knots in their hoses. Knots = Loss!!
  • make sure their knots are secure before scaling a building. Bad Knots = Loss!!
  • make sure to never tie a knot in an oxygen tube. Knots = Loss!!
